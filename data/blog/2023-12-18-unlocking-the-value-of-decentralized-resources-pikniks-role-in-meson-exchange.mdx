We are delighted to announce that PiKNiK has become one of the pioneering suppliers on the Meson Exchange. Part of the inaugural batch of suppliers, PiKNiK is at the forefront of our mission to reshape the way cloud infrastructure resources are managed and exchanged. Originally founded as the first enterprise-tier provider built atop decentralized technologies like IPFS and Filecoin, their expertise in data storage and high performance computing, especially in serving industries like AI, healthcare, education, and media, adds significant value to our global platform. This partnership exemplifies our commitment to enhancing resource efficiency and ensuring secure, decentralized cloud data storage. PiKNiK brings tremendous storage and computational resource capacity based out of multiple U.S. cities. 

The collaboration between PiKNiK and Meson Network's Meson Exchange represents a significant step in addressing the escalating challenges within the bandwidth and bare metal resource markets. This partnership is a response to the pressing demands of these markets and aims to provide innovative solutions. Here are the key highlights of this cooperation:

Resource Synergy: PiKNiK, with its vast array of computing resources, finds a partner in Meson Exchange. By utilizing the Meson Exchange, PiKNiK can seamlessly connect with potential buyers, thereby ensuring their valuable resources are put to productive use, contributing to resource efficiency.

Diverse Network: Meson Exchange's extensive network encompasses a wide range of participants, from suppliers to enterprises. This diversity allows PiKNiK to reach a broader audience of potential buyers, ensuring that their resources are optimally utilized.

Resource Flexibility: Through the current spot market offered by Meson Exchange, PiKNiK can explore a variety of trading options for their idle resources with flexibility. This flexibility empowers PiKNiK to make informed decisions and better plan for the future.

Resource Sustainability: Both PiKNiK and Meson Network are highly committed to resource sustainability. In the face of growing demand for computing resources, this collaboration represents a step towards sustainable resource management. By selling their available resources efficiently, PiKNiK contributes to more responsible use of computing infrastructure.

The collaboration between PiKNiK and Meson Network's Meson Exchange represents a pivotal response to the mounting challenges in the bandwidth and computing resource markets. This partnership serves as an industry beacon, guiding the way for other participants of decentralized infrastructure networks towards addressing the ever-growing complexities in these markets.

We wholeheartedly welcome an increasing number of resource providers and consumers to embrace the Meson Exchange. It is here, within this innovative ecosystem, that ideal partners can seamlessly connect with market demand, ensuring that resources are optimally utilized, and challenges are met with creative and efficient solutions. Together, we embark on a journey to foster resource efficiency, scalability, and the responsible use of technology in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

### **About PiKNiK**

PiKNiK is a Web3 ecosystem multiplier who has dramatically lowered the barriers to entry for both data owners and service providers in pursuit of decentralized networks like IPFS and Filecoin. As the first American storage provider on Filecoin, PiKNiK continues to set the standard for providing cloud products and services to end users atop Web3 storage and compute networks. PiKNiK squarely focuses on serving enterprise-scale clients in data-intensive industries. Today, PiKNiK operates over 200 million gigabytes of data storage and a fleet of modern data center CPUs and GPUs across multiple facilities throughout the United States.

### **About Meson Network**

Meson Network is building the decentralized bandwidth marketplace for Web3, using a blockchain protocol model to replace the traditional labor-based sales models, consolidating and monetizing idle bandwidth from long-tail users at a low cost. Meson Network is the foundation of data transmission for decentralized storage, computation, and the emerging Web3 Dapp ecosystem.
